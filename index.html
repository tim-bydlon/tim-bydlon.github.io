About

As a Senior Manager in Marketing Data Science at Salesforce, I developed and deployed production ML systems including uplift models for personalized GTM campaigns, delivering measurable impact at scale. I was also a Graduate Research Assistant at Georgia Tech's Design Intelligence Lab, where I researched cognitive AI techniques in agentic AI systems. While at the lab I co-authored papers at AIED 2025 and ITS 2025 and deployed an AI product for use in an online Georgia Tech course.

I'm passionate about building intelligent systems that solve real-world problems, whether it's deploying production models that impact millions in revenue or advancing the state of cognitive AI through peer-reviewed research.
